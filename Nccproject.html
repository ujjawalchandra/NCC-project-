About Ncc IIT Roorkee

Building leaders through discipline, unity, and service.

The National Cadet Corps (NCC) at IIT Roorkee functions under the 3 UK CTR Battalion, Roorkee Group. Our unit comprises dedicated cadets committed to fostering leadership.

Since its inception, NCC IIT Roorkee has played a pivotal role in shaping young minds through rigorous training, social service initiatives, and active participation in camps and parades.
